We are committed to making renter's life better in Taipei by providing high-quality and low-priced essentials. Whether you live in a windowless room, a basement, an unapproved rooftop addition, an illegal structure, or on a balcony, you can easily create a safe and beautiful home.

Design Concept

Eight items and eight stories display how to live in Taipei.

The project, "Items Display in Taipei," is an experimental work that combines comics with web design. It pretends to be a brand selling products online. Each item presents the living conditions and depicts the absurdity of life in Taiwan's capital city.
